## The Fan

This document argues the opposite. The blades have definite positions at all times. Measurement is not the creation of a definite state from indefiniteness. Measurement is the act of achieving sufficient resolution to read the state that was always there.

Both images are of the same car. Both are accurate representations. The blur and the frozen dot are not two properties of the car — they are two projections of one path, captured at different temporal resolutions. The duality is in the instrument, not the object.
